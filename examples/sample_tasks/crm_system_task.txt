Phát triển hệ thống CRM (Customer Relationship Management) cho doanh nghiệp vừa và nhỏ với các module:

## Customer Management
- Customer database với thông tin chi tiết
- Customer segmentation và tagging
- Contact history tracking
- Customer lifecycle management
- Import/export customer data
- Duplicate detection và merge

## Lead Management
- Lead capture từ multiple sources
- Lead scoring và qualification
- Lead assignment và routing
- Lead nurturing workflows
- Conversion tracking
- Lead source analytics

## Sales Pipeline
- Opportunity management
- Sales stage tracking
- Deal forecasting
- Quote và proposal generation
- Sales performance metrics
- Territory management

## Communication Tools
- Email integration (Gmail, Outlook)
- Email templates và automation
- SMS integration
- Call logging và recording
- Meeting scheduler
- Communication history

## Reporting & Analytics
- Sales dashboards
- Performance reports
- Revenue analytics
- Customer analytics
- Custom report builder
- Data export capabilities

## Task & Activity Management
- Task creation và assignment
- Activity tracking
- Calendar integration
- Reminder system
- Workflow automation
- Team collaboration tools

## Integration Requirements
- Email service integration
- Calendar integration (Google, Outlook)
- Phone system integration
- Marketing automation tools
- Accounting software integration
- Social media integration

## User Management
- Role-based access control
- User permissions
- Team management
- Activity logging
- Security settings

## Technical Requirements
- Web-based application
- Mobile responsive design
- API development
- Database optimization
- Security implementation
- Backup và recovery

## Advanced Features
- AI-powered insights
- Predictive analytics
- Chatbot integration
- Document management
- E-signature integration
- Multi-language support
